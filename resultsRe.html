Eng 102 - Shakespearean Drama

Introductory study of several genres of English literature, emphasizing both analysis of particular works and the range of forms and styles in English prose and poetry. Frequent writing assignments will be made.

Subject: English Units: 4 Prerequisite: ENL 003 or UWP 001 or UWP 001V or UWP 001Y; Or equivalent.
